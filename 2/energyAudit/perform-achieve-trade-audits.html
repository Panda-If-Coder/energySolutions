What is Perform, Achieve and Trade (PAT)

The Perform Achieve and Trade (PAT) scheme is an initiative of the Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE) under the National Mission for Enhanced Energy Efficiency (NMEEE). It is designed to improve energy efficiency in energy-intensive sectors through market-based mechanisms. The scheme sets specific energy consumption (SEC) targets for designated consumers (DCs) in various sectors. Those who surpass their targets can sell their excess energy savings as Energy Saving Certificates (ESCerts), while those who fail to meet their targets must buy ESCerts to comply.
